Frozen Fury

The sweetest tower defense you'll found.

Previous Next

Frozen fury is an action and strategy videogame where the player must defend a main objective from different hordes of enemies. To do this, the player will be able to move his character around the map freely and defend himself from enemies both by building structures around the map and by automatically shooting directly at the enemies that appear. For each map, there will be a certain number of rounds to beat and, once these are completed, the player will be able to beat his own personal best. If the main objective is defeated, the game will be over.

LORE

A young ice cream maker is trying to create the best homemade ice cream in the world, but he feels that today's industrial ingredients don’t allow him to create the ultimate homemade ice cream. He then decides to tune his ice cream truck and turn it into a time machine to visit Leonardo da Vinci and steal his notebook, where it is said that he once described the final ingredient for the Gelato of God, an ice cream so majestic that it allowed to see what the sky was like. However, what he didn't expect his time machine to be defective and instead of taking him directly to his destination, it made him travel through different times and universes until he finally arrived in Renaissance Italy. In this journey, the ice cream man will steal different resources to create his ultimate ice cream, finding that the people he steals it from will not allow him to get away with it so easily...

Characters

Ice cream man : a wacky young man who’s on a quest to create the ultimate homemade ice cream and, to do so, he creates a time machine to steal from Leonardo da Vinci the ingredient he has written in his notebook.

ConeMan : A crazy ice cream man who claims that ice cream should always be eaten in a cone, a bitter enemy of Torrine, he usually eats his ice cream in one bite because that way he ensures that it is much more enjoyable. It is possible that by doing this so many times and suffering from brain freezes, He has become a little demented.

Torrine : A cocky girl who ensure that ice cream is a product of nobility and, as such, should be eaten delicately in a terrine. She’s been ConeMan's enemy since they met because of their different ideologies.
